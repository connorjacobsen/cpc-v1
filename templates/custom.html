In addition to being used as statement stuffers, these pieces are perfect for many other uses, including flyers, leaflets, and trade-show handouts. We offer five paper stocks: 70# uncoated text, 80# gloss text, 100# uncoated cover (14pt), 120# gloss cover (12pt) and 120# dull/matte cover (12pt). Printing is 4-color process on one side with your choice of 4-color, black or blank on the other side. Aqueous Coating is optional.

CD Covers and inlay cards are printed as one item on industry standard 100# gloss text paper. The inlay card is perforated at the folds for easy insertion into a CD case. They are printed 4 color on one side and your choice of 4 color, black or blank on the other with optional aqueous coating.
